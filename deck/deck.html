THE PROBLEM
Guardian platforms publish
environmental credits in silos
6 Platforms, No Aggregation
DOVU, Tolam Earth, Capturiant, OrbexCO2, Global Carbon Registry, and TYMLEZ each publish tokens to Hedera independently. No cross-platform aggregation layer exists yet.
No Agent Standard — Yet
AI agents have no machine-readable standard for discovering, valuing, or verifying environmental data across Guardian platforms. The schemas and feeds don't exist yet.
Not Yet Discoverable
80,674 tCO2e of environmental actions ingested from 6 Guardian platforms. The broader ecosystem — agents, protocols, capital allocators — can't yet discover them as a unified dataset.
The raw data is on Hedera. The intelligence layer is the next opportunity.
